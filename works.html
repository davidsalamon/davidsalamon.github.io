Wind Light

Edition: 100

Wind Light is a one-of-a-kind designer lamp. The poetic luminaire is produced in a limited edition, where each example is unique.

It is inspired by the forces of nature, forming the physical embodiment of an intangible, digital sensation.

In designing it, I was looking for an answer to the question: how might we leverage the characteristics of 3D printing to our advantage in a collectible design object?

LetThereBe Light

Edition: 1/1

For LetThereBe Light, we wanted to evoke a sense of sacrality through electronics.

The luminaire combines the functions of both candleholder and mood lighting: it uses its built-in digital camera to monitor the candle flame and electronically multiplies it.

The mesmerising play of the flame is projected in real time onto the wall, creating an elevated yet cosy atmosphere.

Co-designers: Enikő Juhász and Daniel Ferenczi

Bloomaire Kinetic Chandelier

Edition: Limited series

The large-scale luminaire was commissioned by Rossana Orlandi from Diana Alchhabi and LumoConcept. By creating an elegant motion sequence, we designed the kinetics to bring the idea to life.

As the kinetic lighting designer on the team, I was responsible for the motion concept, electronic design, motors, sensors and electronics.

Design concept: Diana Alchhabi
Principal designer: András Jánosi
Mechanical designer: Szabolcs Poller-Nagy
Kinetic designer: David Salamon
