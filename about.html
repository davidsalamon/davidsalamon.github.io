Artist statement

The motto "Amaze, Delight, and Inspire" guides every aspect of my work.

As a designer-artist, I craft collectible, one-of-a-kind lamps that bring magic and individuality to exclusive interiors.
These creations are more than functional objects; they are works of art, designed to captivate both the imagination and the intellect.
Inspired by the fusion of poetry and technology, I aim to evoke reflection, spark curiosity, and leave a lasting impression.

Through the thoughtful interplay of shapes, materials, and innovation, I strive to create timeless pieces that transform spaces into extraordinary environments.
These lamps are meant to be cherished not only for their functionality but also for their ability to provoke thought and stir emotion.

With a background in both art and engineering, I embrace the belief that art and functionality are not opposing forces but complementary ones.
My designs bring these elements together, creating unique, purposeful objects that resonate deeply with those who encounter them.
